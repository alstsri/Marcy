marcy — privacy policy

Last updated: April 2026
Marcy stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your health data.

Data Storage

All cycle tracking data — including period dates, tension events, and settings — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Email Collection

If you voluntarily provide your email address, it is submitted to Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form handling service, for the sole purpose of sending product updates. Your email is not linked to your health data in any way. You may request removal of your email by contacting us.

Health Data

Marcy does not integrate with Apple Health, HealthKit, or any other health data framework. No health data is collected, transmitted, or shared.

Analytics and Tracking

Marcy does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, cookies, or any form of user tracking.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting the app. No server-side data exists to delete, with the exception of voluntarily submitted email addresses.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is Formspree (formspree.io) for optional email collection. No other third-party services receive any data from the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: hello@marcy.app
